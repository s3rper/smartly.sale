### Ad Performance Benchmarks

**Good Performance**:
- CTR (Link Click-Through Rate): 1-2%
- CPC (Cost Per Click): ₱3-8
- CPM (Cost Per 1000 Impressions): ₱50-150
- Relevance Score: 7+

**Needs Improvement**:
- CTR: <1%
- CPC: >₱10
- CPM: >₱200
- Relevance Score: <6

### Optimization Tips

1. **Test Multiple Audiences**: Run 3-4 ad sets simultaneously
2. **Creative Testing**: 3-5 ad variations per ad set
3. **Budget Allocation**: Give top performers 60% of budget
4. **Kill Poor Performers**: Pause ads with CTR <0.8% after 1000 impressions
5. **Refresh Creative**: New images/videos every 7-14 days

**Button Text A/B Testing Results**:

## 💳 Display Ad Monetization

### Google AdSense Setup

**Requirements**:
- Original content (✓)
- Privacy policy page (✓)
- About page (✓)
- Contact page (✓)
- Minimum 20 posts
- Clean, professional design

**Best Ad Placements**:
1. **Top Banner** (728x90 or 970x90)
   - Above content, below header
   - CPM: ₱50-100

2. **Sidebar** (300x600 or 160x600)
   - Desktop only
   - CPM: ₱80-150

3. **In-Content** (336x280 or 300x250)
   - After 2-3 paragraphs
   - CPM: ₱100-200 (highest)

4. **Bottom** (728x90)
   - Before footer
   - CPM: ₱30-60

**Expected Revenue**:
- 10K monthly visitors: ₱5,000-10,000
- 50K monthly visitors: ₱25,000-50,000
- 100K monthly visitors: ₱60,000-120,000

### Ezoic (After 10K Visitors)

**Benefits**:
- AI-powered ad optimization
- 2-3x higher RPM than AdSense
- Access to premium ad networks
- Detailed analytics

**Setup**:
1. Apply at ezoic.com
2. Integrate with website
3. Let AI test placements
4. Monitor for 30 days
